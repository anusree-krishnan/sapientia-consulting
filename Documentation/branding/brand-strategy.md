# Sapientia Brand Strategy

## Name

Sapientia is derived from the Latin word for wisdom.

## Mission

To empower organizations through secure, intelligent, and sustainable technology transformation.

## Core Pillars

- Cybersecurity
- Cloud Transformation
- AI & Automation
- Strategic Technology Consulting

## Brand Personality

- Intelligent
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Analytical
- Human-Centered

## Design Inspiration

The visual identity combines classical Greco-Roman themes with modern consulting aesthetics to symbolize timeless wisdom applied to contemporary technological challenges.
